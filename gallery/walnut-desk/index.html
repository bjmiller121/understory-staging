Writing Desk with Drawers

This solid walnut desk has a three-drawer cabinet, including a large bottom drawer to support hanging folders. The top surface of the cabinet acts as a shelf to store frequently used items while keeping the desktop clear. Ideally sized for writing, drawing, or working on a laptop, this desk provides an excellent workstation for staying organized and getting things done.

Materials: Black Walnut, Baltic Birch Plywood (drawer boxes)

Dimensions: 50" Wide by 23" Deep by 30" Tall
