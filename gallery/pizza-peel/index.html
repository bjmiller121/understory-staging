Cherry and Walnut Pizza Peel

This cherry and walnut pizza peel makes transferring homemade pizzas to and from a pizza stone a breeze. The parts are book-matched to display mirrored symmetry in the grain pattern. This very functional piece also looks great hung up in the kitchen when not in use.

Materials: Cherry, Walnut

Dimensions: 15" Wide by 0.375" Deep by 29" Tall
