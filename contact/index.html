Contact

Interested in my work or considering comissioning some custom furniture? Send me a message below and I'll get back to you as soon as possible. Or scroll a little bit further to see some frequently asked questions.







FAQ

"Can you tell me the price for [X]?"

Cost depends on a number of factors including wood species, size of the piece, and complexity. I can typically provide a quick estimate for existing work. Custom work with new designs take some more discussion before I can accurately provide an estimate. Shoot me a message with what you have in mind and I'll be happy to discuss options and provide estimates.

"Do you take commissions? What sorts of projects will you accept?"

Yes, I primarily do commission work, with a focus on furniture. Have another woodworking project in mind? Chances are I can help with that as well.

"Do you ship or deliver?"

Furniture: I can provide free delivery within a couple hours drive of Seattle, Washington. Local pickup is always an option as well. At the moment, I don't ship furniture outside the Seattle area. If you're not in my area, I encourage you to find a woodworker local to your area to work with to support your local economy and small business owners. If you aren't local, but really want a particular piece of mine, shoot me a message and we can discuss options.

Smaller Items: I can ship smaller items such as boxes and wall shelves within the United States. Shipping costs depend on size and shipping method.

"What's your design process like?"

My design process is flexible to suit your needs. Whether you have a clear vision of what you want built or just a general idea, I can work with you from concepts to a final design. I do much of my design work with 3D modeling software that allows me to iterate quickly on designs and provide renderings from any angle.

If you see a piece I've made on this website or my Instagram, I'm happy to build you that piece in your wood of choice and desired dimensions.

"What are other ways I can reach you?"

Feel free to email me directly at hello@understorywoodworking.com.
